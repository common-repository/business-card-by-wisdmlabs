An easy to use customizable Business Card Plugin for displaying your visiting card on any WordPress page.

== Description ==

WordPress Business Card by WisdmLabs is an easy to use, customizable plugin for designing and displaying your business/visiting card on your WordPress website. All you have to do is just enter your data in the 'Business Card' settings page, customize the background & font color using the settings provided, preview your visiting card as you make changes, click on save and voila! it's done.
Then just put the shortcode [visiting_card] on the page where you want to display your business card.

== Installation ==

How to install '__Business Card by WisdmLabs__'

= Method 1 -Installing from the WordPress: =
1. From the Dashboard, locate the 'Plugins'. Select 'Add New' under Plugin tab.
2. Search for the term __WordPress Business Card__
3. Select for the __WordPress Business Card__ on the Results page and click 'Install Now' button under it.
4. After the installation is finished, click 'Activate Plugin'. Once it is activated you would be able to see 'Business Card' Tab in Menu page.
5. Now go the 'Business Card' page for creating your Business Card.
6. Fill in the information and customize your card by choosing the colors you like.
7. When done click on 'Save settings'.
8. Use the shortcode [visiting_card] to display your business card.

= Method 2- Installing Manually : =
1. Download the 'wordpress-business-card.zip'file.
2. After the download has completed, go to your WordPress dashboard.
3. From the Dashboard, locate the 'Plugins'. Select 'Add New' under Plugin tab.
4. On that page, click on the 'Upload' Tab.
5. It would open a new window. On that window, click 'Browse' button and locate 'wordpress-business-card.zip'. After locating the file, select it and click 'Open' button and then press 'Install now'.
6. It might ask you for the FTP details before uploading. Kindly, fill those fields and install the plugin.
7. After the installation is finished, click 'Activate Plugin'. Once it is activated you would be able to see 'Business Card' Tab in Menu page.
5. Now go the 'Business Card' page for creating your Business Card.
6. Fill in the information and customize your card by choosing the colors you like.
7. When done click on 'Save settings'.
8. Use the shortcode [visiting_card] to display your business card.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How should I display the visiting card that I customized in the settings page on any of my wordpress page ? =
To display your visiting card simply go into the page editor (WYSIWYG) of your desired page and put the shortcode [visiting_card] wherever you want to display your card.
